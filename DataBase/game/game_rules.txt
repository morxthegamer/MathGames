In the easy game mode, your starting range can be from 1-10 and your ending range can be from 10-20.

MEDIUM GAME MODE:

HARD GAME MODE:
In the hard game mode, your starting range can be from 40-80 and your ending range can be from 80-160.

HARDER GAME MODE:
In the harder game mode, your starting range can be from 100-150 and your ending range can be from 160-280.

EXTREME GAME MODE:
In the extreme game mode, your starting range can be from 200 and up and your ending range can be from 320 and up.
